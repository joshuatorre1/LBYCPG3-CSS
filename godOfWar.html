ABOUT THE GAME

God of War is an action-adventure developed by Santa Monica Studio and published by Sony Interactive Entertainment. It is the eighth installment in the God of War series set in ancient Scandinavia in the realm of Midgard. It follows Kratos, the former Greek God of War, and his son, Atreus, as they journey to fulfill his wife's request to spread her ashes from the highest peak of the nine realms.

Kratos

The former Greek God of War, after exacting his vengeance on his father, the Olympians, and the Titans he escaped into the world of Norse Gods. Settling down in Midgard, he and his wife had a son named Atreus who, after his mother's death, would embark on a journey alongside him to spread her ashes from the highest peak of all the nine realms.

  • Leviathan Axe
  • Guardian Shield
  • Blades of Chaos

Atreus

Son of Kratos and Faye, he was a sickly child who had only recently grew healthy enough to travel. He constantly tries to prove that he can handle himself as it is implied that he feels unwanted by his father and that this sense of rejection is what drives him to become a better warrior.

  • Talon Bow
  • Atreus' Knife

Baldur

The Norse Aesir God if Light and the main antagonist of the story. After his birth, his mother learned that he would die a "needless death". Determined to prevent his death at any cost, she placed a spell on him that made him invulnerable to all threats, physical or magical. It had the side-effect of removing Baldur's ability to physically feel anything which caused him to resent his mother and to eventually seek freedom from it.

  • True Invulnerability
  • Light Manipulation
  • Additional Elemental Affinities

Freya

Known as the Witch of the Woods, she is a Vanir goddess who helps Kratos and Atreus in their journey. She is very friendly, hospitable, and at times motherly towards Atreus and occasionally scolds Kratos for keeping his past a secret from Atreus.

  • Spellcasting Mastery
  • Gifted Intellect

Mimir

Nicknamed as the Smartest Man Alive, he was Odin's advisor and the ambassador of the Aesir gods until he was imprisoned 109 years ago. He is well-mannered, kind, and possesses cheery yet occasionally sarcastic sense of humor and serves as an advisor and teacher to Kratos and Atreus throughout their journey.

  • Vast Wisdom
  • Magic Eyes

Sindri

A drawf who along with his brother, Brok, were responsible for forging Mjolnir and the Leviathan Axe. He is polite, well-meaning and is extremely squeamish with anything that could be considered unclean.

  • Master Inventor
  • Dwarven Magic

Brok

A drawf who along with his brother, Sindri, were responsible for forging Mjolnir and the Leviathan Axe. He is often rude and ill-mannered but is generally good-natured and comes to care for Kratos and Atreus.

  • Master Inventor
  • Dwarven Magic

Magni

The son of Thor and half-brother of Modi, he is arrogant, sadistic, and bloodthirsty. Out of the two, he appears to be the smartest, or at the very least, the less impulsive and immature one.

  • Electrokinesis
  • Cyclonic Blade

Modi

The son of Thor and half-brother of Magni, he is extremely rude, vulgar, and impulsive. He is desperate to please his father and earn the right to wield his hammer due to Thor's favoritism towards Magni.

  • Electrokinesis
  • Mace and Shield

HOW TO PLAY


  • Kratos can attack in two different ways, regardless if he's using his fists or one of his weapons, which are classified as light or heavy attacks

  • Staggered enemies are prone to finishers so be on the lookout for dazed enemies to trigger a takedown

  • When Spartan Rage meter is glowing, activate it to unleash a devastating frenzy mode that dishes out huge damage

  • Kratos can defend himself in 3 ways: Blocking, Dodging, and Parrying

  • By default, Atreus will target the enemy Kratos is locked onto however you can command him to fire at a specific target. As Atreus progresses throughout the story, he will gain access to new arrow typesand subdue enemies with melee attacks

  • Upgrade your weapons, armor, and talismans in order to enhance your core stats of: Strength, Runic, Defense, Luck, Vitality, and Cooldown

  • Throughout the journey you will come across runes which hold runic abilities that augment Kratos and Atreus' weapons with special moves and abilities

  • Kratos and Atreus have skill trees which allow you to add or upgrade attacks, follow-ups, and techniques
