ABOUT THE GAME

Persona 5 Royal is a RPG developed by Atlus and is the sixth installment in the Persona series. It takes place in modern day Tokyo and follows a high school student known as Joker who transfers to a new school after being falsely accused. Over the course of the year, he and other students awaken to a special power, becoming the vigilantes known as the Phantom Thieves of Hearts

Joker

He is the leader of the Phantom Thieves of Hearts, a group who steals the hearts of corrupt adults in order to reform society. His personality is based on the player's actions and decisions. He is a second-year high school student who transfers to Shujin academy after being falsely accused of assault.

HOW TO PLAY


  • You can perform activities throughout the day which can be used to: boost stats, increase Confidant relationships, or gather materials for your next exploration

  • Boosting stats is important as these stats (Knowledge, Guts, Charm, Proficiency, and Kindness) will impact your ability to do certain things

  • Increase your relationships with Confidants as it affects: your ability to create Personas, a character's passive abilities in combat, and options for romance

  • The combat is turn based with the order being determined by the Speed attribute of the characters and enemies. Each character has a melee attack, gun attack, and Persona skills which vary from one another

  • Only the protagonist has the ability to gain, fuse, and switch Personas. Personas can be gained throughout the story and can be upgraded and fused in the Velvet Room

  • For each Palace, there is a deadline for the latest it can be tackled. Failure to steal the treasure before then results in a game over.

  • Requests are obtained through either the Phansite or Mishima. These requests have you go into Mementos to track down Shadows who are causing people problems but are not distorted enough to create a Palace
